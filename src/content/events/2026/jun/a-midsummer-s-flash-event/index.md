Don your finest cloak and let your fae flag fly!

Come and revel with us at our upcoming immersive art flash event. Many designs for flash are available for claim while slots last, or draw your own fancies on our community canvas of dreams! 

Food, drink, and all manner of ethereal activities can be enjoyed as well: get your fortune told by way of oracle cards or osteomancy (bone reading). Make a flower crown! Bring your kids for coloring activities and face paint! Or just pop in and enjoy the fantastical ambience.

Costumes are optional, but HIGHLY ENCOURAGED! It's time to dust off that fairy crown or jester hat sitting in the closet, passed down in your family for generations from the days of yore.

**Come dressed up and receive a Special Gift!**
