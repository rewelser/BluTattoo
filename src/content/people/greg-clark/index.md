Greg grew up in a tattoo shop and knew from an early age that was what he wanted to do. With a background in art, professional sports, and blue collar work he brings that work ethic with him into the tattoo industry and has quickly become a well rounded artist that's up to do anything you throw at him. 

Whether it's American, Neo or Japanese Traditional, Anime/Cartoons, Black and Grey, Horror, Bands/Logos, Fine Line, Floral, Script, Illustrative, Cybersigilism, Trash Polka, New School, Blackouts and Black Work, Black and Grey Stiple work and Animal Realism etc. He doesn't believe in boxing himself into a category as a "specialist" as he's willing and able to do just about anything you can think of. His favorites are definitely anything vibrant and colorful like New School, Anime/Cartoons, Illustrative and American/Neo/Japanese Traditional but has really started to find a passion for Black and Grey realism and Horror Portraits as well. 

If you're looking for a hard working versatile artist that'll put his all into your tattoo while keeping you entertained and laughing the entire time, consider checking him out as well as giving him a follow [@madtatter91](https://www.tiktok.com/@madtatter91) on TikTok where he shares all of his work!
