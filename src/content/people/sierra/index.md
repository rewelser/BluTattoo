Sierra started pursuing her tattoo journey in 2019, and what a ride it has been!

Continuously exposed to tattoo culture since the age of 13, she had the opportunity to teach herself the craft and turn her passion into a successful career as a professional tattoo artist. She found her home at BluTattoo, where she has proudly been working for over three years and counting.

Sierra’s style is constantly evolving. She enjoys creating neo-traditional, realism, Japanese traditional, fine-line, and floral tattoos, and she plans to further specialize in realistic pet portraits.
