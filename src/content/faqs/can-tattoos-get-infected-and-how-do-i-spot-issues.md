---
name: Can tattoos get infected and how do I spot issues?
section: aftercare-healing
sort: 23
---

Tattoos are open wounds that can get infected if not properly taken care of. If you notice any persistent inflammation in or around the tattoo, persistent pain, thick yellow or green pus, or foul odors, you should seek medical attention. If you are unsure of infection, contact your artist and they can help direct you on how you should proceed.
