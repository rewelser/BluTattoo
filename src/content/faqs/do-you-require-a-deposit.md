section: pricing-booking

The cost of tattoos depends on each artist's hourly rate. Our senior artists, Lara and Laurna, with 10+ years of experience, charge $150 and hour while our junior artists, Greg and Sierra, with 2+ years of experience, charge $125 an hour, and our last junior artist, Zoe, with 1 year of experience, is $100 an hour. 

We recommend talking with your artist to get a quote of how much a piece would cost because everyone's skill, speed, and styles are different.
