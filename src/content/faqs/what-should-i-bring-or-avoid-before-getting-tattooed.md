---
name: What should I bring (or avoid) before getting tattooed?
section: process-expectations
sort: 15
---

Bring your ID, water, snacks, method of payment, comfortable clothing you don't mind getting stained with ink or blood, and a clean, calm, well rested you! Some people like to bring a friend or partner for support which is fine by us. Avoid drinking, the tattoo area getting sunburned or injured (scraped or bruised), having excessive caffeine, and not getting enough rest before your tattoo.
