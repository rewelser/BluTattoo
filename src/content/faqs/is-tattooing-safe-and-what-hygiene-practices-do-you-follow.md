---
name: Is tattooing safe, and what hygiene practices do you follow?
section: safety-policies
sort: 19
---

Yes, tattooing is safe when done in an up-to-date, licensed business. In Virginia, we are licensed under DPOR (Department of Professional and Occupational Regulation) and follow the laws and regulations they set. For hygiene, every artist in our studio uses single-use needle cartridges that are put into a sharps container after use. Once these sharps containers are full, they are closed and a service collects them and disposes of them. Anything that is not thrown away after a client's appointment is cleaned thoroughly with Madacide, which is a hospital-grade EPA registered disinfectant.
