---
name: What activities should I avoid after getting a tattoo (showering, swimming, working out, sun exposure)?
section: aftercare-healing
sort: 18
---

Avoid submerging your tattoo in water(taking a bath, hot tub, swimming) for 6 weeks until your tattoo is fully healed. You should also avoid working out for about a week after getting your tattoo, unless your tattoo is around a high-movement area such as a joint where you should avoid working out for around 2 weeks to avoid over-stretching/over-using the skin.

View our [aftercare page](/aftercare) for more in-depth info.
