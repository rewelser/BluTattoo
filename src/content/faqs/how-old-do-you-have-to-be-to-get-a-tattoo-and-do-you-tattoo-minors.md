---
name: How old do you have to be to get a tattoo (and do you tattoo minors)?
section: safety-policies
sort: 20
---

In the state of Virginia, we tattoo 16+ with guardian permission and 18+ without. If you are the guardian of a minor looking to get a tattoo, please bring your ID, your child's ID, and a birth certificate to complete the consent form for their appointment.
