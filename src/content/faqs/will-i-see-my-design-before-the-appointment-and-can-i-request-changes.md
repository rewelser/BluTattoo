---
name: Will I see my design before the appointment, and can I request changes?
section: design-artist-selection
sort: 11
---

This will vary by artist, most will not be comfortable showing you the final design until you are face to face. If your artist is comfortable sharing sketches and ideas with you before your appointment you are ENCOURAGED to make requests and changes if you feel the need. Your tattoo will be on your skin for the rest of your life, we want you to be happy with your art.
